About

Are you asking yourself, where I can have some fun and where is going to be a party?
Isolation finished, party time's beginning! So where to go when finally all the places will be open and everyone could enjoy the weekend.
All that information you can find on our website, WE LOVE MUSIC will show you the most famous places in London.


Many new nightclubs appear in the capital every year, but few of them pass the test of time. And only the very most become favourite vacation spots of both Londoners and tourists.
The best clubs in London are, first of all, high-quality musical space and an interesting audience. The menu in such establishments contains a wide range of drinks and dishes that support a high degree of positive mood. The most important criterion for the best club is a stylish interior, including a modern light and musical design. It is equally good to hold concerts of well-known musical groups and have incendiary parties with dances until the morning.
Nightclubs are, by definition, a place where the atmosphere of positive, music and lights reigns. But among the huge number of such establishments, it is important to choose the best, so as not to spoil the mood and not spend precious time moving from one place to another in search of a real holiday.

Do you want to get to a grand party and spend time surrounded by crowds or dance by high-quality tracks? Maybe there is a desire to plunge into the atmosphere of luxury and glamor?
Only the best places in London will show you WE LOVE MUSIC for every taste for an unforgettable weekend.
